Need help with RoleLogic? We're here to assist you. Choose the option that best fits your needs.

Before reaching out, try these resources—many questions are answered here:

- **[Quick Start Guide](./quick-start)** — Get up and running fast

Join our community Discord server for:

**[Join the Support Server →](#)** _(Link available on the RoleLogic website)_

### What to Include When Asking for Help

For the fastest assistance, please provide:

1. **Server name or ID** (if comfortable sharing)
2. **What you expected to happen**
3. **What actually happened**
4. **Steps you've already tried**
5. **Screenshots** (if helpful)

Example:

> "I created a rule to add VIP when someone gets Server Booster, but nothing happens. I've verified the bot has Manage Roles permission and the role hierarchy looks correct. Here's a screenshot of my rule configuration..."

## Common Support Topics

### Setup Issues

- **Bot won't respond**: Check if RoleLogic is online and in your server
- **Can't see my server**: Verify you have Administrator or Manage Roles permission
- **Permission errors**: Review [Role Hierarchy](./concepts/role-hierarchy) documentation

### Rule Issues

- **Rule not triggering**: Check the [FAQ troubleshooting section](./faq#troubleshooting)
- **Unexpected behavior**: Test in the [Sandbox](./features/testing-sandbox) first
- **Rules conflicting**: Check rule priorities and conditions

### Billing & Premium

- **Subscription questions**: Managed through Patreon
- **Sync delays**: Premium status syncs within 5 minutes
- **Refunds**: Contact Patreon support (available within 7 days)

## Contact Options

### General Support

For most questions, the community Discord server is the fastest option. Our team and experienced users monitor it regularly.

### Account Issues

For account-specific concerns (data deletion, privacy questions, account access):

- Reach out through the support server
- Include your Discord username/ID for verification

### Business Inquiries

For enterprise plans, partnerships, or business matters:

- Contact us through official channels listed on the website
- Include relevant details about your organization and needs

### Bug Reports

Found a bug? Help us fix it:

1. Check if it's already reported in the support server
2. Provide detailed reproduction steps
3. Include screenshots or videos if possible
4. Note your browser and any relevant settings

### Feature Requests

We love hearing ideas:

1. Share in the support server's feedback channel
2. Describe the problem the feature would solve
3. Explain how you envision it working
4. Be open to discussion—others may have input

## Tips for Getting Help Faster

### Do

- ✅ Search documentation first
- ✅ Check the FAQ
- ✅ Provide specific details
- ✅ Include screenshots when relevant
- ✅ Be patient and respectful

### Don't

- ❌ Ask the same question in multiple channels
- ❌ DM team members without permission
- ❌ Expect instant responses
- ❌ Be vague ("it doesn't work")

## Service Status

If you suspect RoleLogic is experiencing issues:

1. **Check Discord's status** at [status.discord.com](https://status.discord.com) — Discord outages affect all bots
2. **Check our support server** for announcements about known issues
3. **Wait and retry** — temporary issues often resolve quickly
